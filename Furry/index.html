Can I Use ?

This is actually an explaination about why I write a paragraph to talk about license. Since I am a fake FSF believer or kinda freedom seeker, I hope you can use these artworks or character without any worry if you would like to follow the license associating to that artwork or character.

That’s a license, so it means it’s not totally a “Do WTF I Wanna Do” license, or say, there are some limits. Some are from the artist who draw the artwork, some are write by myself. I will make the license reasonable and human-readable, see texts below.

License for Artworks

TL,DR:

I will tag the license which the artwork use so you can see the license when you see the artwork from this site.

Explain:

Artist have their freedom to choose a license they like, so whatever they choice, that’s their freedom, we should always respect their choice.

I’ll trying ask for a clearly license for an artwork/commission and try to get at least a CC BY-NC-ND. If I forgot or I finally can’t get a clearly usage explicate, you’d better not use that artwork.

License for Character

TL,DR:

CC BY-NC-ND

Explain:

Actually not that strict, someone who is to lazy to read the full text explain should use that strict license. So let’s talk about these limits and can you get rid of it.

  • BY: You can’t get rid of this limit. I think it’s reasonable to credit me if you wanna use my stuff.
  • NC: Yep it depends. If you are an indie developer or a hobbyist and wanna use the character in your project, you can use the character to make money. But, BUT! You MUST have your creative inside your project, not just take a copy and counting dollars. So, if you wanna get rid of the NC limit, make sure you are ONE person, and there are your own creative in your project. If you are not sure about this, send me a message on twitter~
  • ND: Actually not that limit. Since OC or called Fursona always act as a identifier of a person inside “Furry Fandom” or something, so I don’t want anyone take the character and draw nsfw artwork or something like this. If you did, just don’t release.
